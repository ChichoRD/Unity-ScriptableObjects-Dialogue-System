Dialogue System Tags:

*All numeric values support decimals with "," or "."


<w, INTENSITY>Wavy text</w>							Recommended: INTENSITY (5, 15)*

<s, INTENSITY>Shaky text</s>						Recommended: INTENSITY (0.1, 2f)*

<#HEXCODE>Colored text</#>							Supports Hexadecimal: (6x rr gg bb) and (8x rr gg bb aa)

<e, EVENTNAME>										Called when current phrase is parsed: Asign Calls in Inspector
													through DialogueUI component

{variable}											Substituted when parsed: Asign the variable with name between 
													curly brackets by code, methods implemented in DIalogueUI component
